## About the Game

Han Seoyun quit her job at a black company — and on that very day, she meets an unjust death at the hands of corruption in the afterlife.

To escape death and return to the living, Seoyun infiltrates the afterlife as a Grim Reaper, with help from the Grim Reaper Hyeon...

**But soul-crushing work even in the afterlife?!**

## Comment

A romantic comedy visual novel blended with black comedy, tackling the very Korean issues of workplace exploitation and death from overwork.

Developed over one month for the 'SWAK Jam 2025' game jam on itch.io.
