## Comment

This game was developed in Unity over the course of a single day — July 21st — for two game jams with strict creative constraints. The result has the following characteristics:
- Text under 1,000 characters (in both Korean and English)
- 1 character image (with facial expressions)
- 1 background image
- 1 piece of music
- 1 sound effect
- 1 choice
