## About the Game

* Full-screen or downloaded play is recommended!

As the "Robot Manager", control robots to uncover the truth behind the incident aboard a spaceship and evacuate the crew.
An experimental nonlinear mystery puzzle inspired by *Return of the Obra Dinn* and *Outer Wilds*.
