## About the Game

There are 3 secrets hidden in this game (and beyond).
Secret 1. This will come in handy for speedrunning and multiple playthroughs.
Secret 2. There is a hidden button that can keep a secret.
Secret 3. Who are you? Look for the secret of 'your' identity — both 'in' and 'outside' the game.

## Comment

This game was developed over two weeks for GitHub's Game Off 2024 game jam.

The jam's theme was "Secret." To fully embody this theme, a "Secret" stat exists within gameplay — but secrets are also hidden within the game world and beyond.

In particular, try combining information from 'inside' and 'outside' the game to uncover the secret.

It won't be easy, but you'll come to understand the story and secret behind the protagonist.
